General Questions

What is TUS Horizons?

Horizons is a digital gallery platform where students can showcase projects, employers can browse talent, and staff can review student work.

Do I need an account to view projects?

No. Visitors can browse public showcases without logging in. Students and employers must log in to access dashboards and additional features.

Who can upload projects?

Only authenticated students with valid TUS accounts can upload and manage their own projects.

How do I contact support?

You can reach the platform team through the Contact page.
